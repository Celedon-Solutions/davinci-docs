---
title: Quick Start Guide
---
This guide will teach you how to get up and running with Davinci in minutes.

## 1. Log In or Create an Account
Go to [https://davinci-app.com](https://davinci-app.com). If you are not signed in, you will be directed to the login page.
- If you already have an account, enter your email and password to sign in.
- If you do **not** already have an account, enter your email and follow the prompts to create a password and verify your email address. You can also sign in with Google or SSO if available for your organization.

## 2. Create a New Project
After signing in, you will land on the Home page. Click the `New` button in the sidebar to create a project.

<Frame>
    <img height="400" noZoom src="/images/home_start_click.png" /> 
</Frame>

In the dialog that appears, enter a **Project Name**, choose an **Owner**, and select a **Visibility** level. Then click `Create Project`.

 <Frame>
    <img height="100" noZoom src="/images/home_project_def.png" /> 
</Frame>

Your new project will appear in the sidebar. Click on it to open its Project Page, then click `Open in Editor` to load it in the design workspace.

 <Frame>
    <img height="400" src="/images/home_project_open.png" /> 
</Frame>

## 3. Execute your First Task
Once you are in the Editor, you can begin creating your Model. Go to the Chat Bar at the bottom and try asking Davinci to build a simple parts tree. Press Enter to send messages to Davinci.

<Frame>
    <img height="400" src="/images/chat_input_example.png" /> 
</Frame>

## 4. Review the Results
Once the task is complete, you will see new objects populated in the Model Index. You can interact directly with the objects by clicking on them in the index. If you want to focus specifically on what Davinci created during that task, you can click on the `View` button in the Chat response to see a change log of the modifications. 

<Frame>
    <img height="400" src="/images/chat_example.png" /> 
</Frame>

**Happy Modeling!**
