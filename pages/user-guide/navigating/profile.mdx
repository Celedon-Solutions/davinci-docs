Every Davinci user has a public profile page. Your profile gives other users a way to see who you are, what projects you have shared publicly, and your recent activity.

## Accessing Your Profile

Your profile is available at `davinci-app.com/@{your-handle}`. You can reach it by:
- Clicking your avatar in the top bar and selecting **Profile**
- Sharing the URL directly with others

Your handle is displayed on your profile page below your name. It is assigned when your account is created.

## Profile Page

Your profile page has a header showing your **display name**, **avatar**, and **handle**. Below the header are three tabs:

### Home
The default tab. It shows:
- **Project count** — the total number of projects associated with your account
- **Member since** — the date you joined Davinci
- **Recent Projects** — a list of your most recent public projects. Click a project to go to its Project Page. If you have more than five projects, a "View all projects" link appears.
- **Recent Activity** — a feed of your recent commits across projects, with links to the associated project pages. A "View all" link leads to the full Activity tab.

### Projects
A full list of your public projects. Each entry shows the project name, visibility badge, and when it was last updated. Click any project to navigate to its Project Page.

### Activity
A complete feed of your recent activity across public projects, including commit messages, branches, and timestamps.

## What Others See

Other users who visit your profile can see your public projects and activity. Private projects are not shown to other users. The profile is read-only — visitors cannot modify your projects or settings from your profile page.

## Editing Your Profile

Your display name and avatar color can be changed from your [Account Settings](https://davinci-app.com/~/settings) under the General tab.
