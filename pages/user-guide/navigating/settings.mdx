Your Account Settings page is where you manage everything about your Davinci account. Access it at [https://davinci-app.com/~/settings](https://davinci-app.com/~/settings), or by clicking your avatar in the top bar and selecting **Settings**.

Settings are organized into five tabs: **General**, **Billing**, **Connections**, **Storage**, and **About**.

## General

The General tab covers your profile, security, and preferences.

### Account

- **Name** — your display name, shown across Davinci and on your public profile. Click the pencil icon to edit it.
- **Avatar Color** — the background color of your avatar bubble. Click `Randomize` to pick a new color.
- **Email** — the email address tied to your account. This is read-only and cannot be changed from this page.
- **License Key** — a unique identifier for your account. It is hidden by default; click the eye icon to reveal it. Support may ask for this when helping with your account.
- **Password** — click `Change Password` to update your password. This option is only shown if you signed up with email (not available for Google or SSO sign-in).
- **Sign Out** — log out of Davinci on this browser.

### Handle

Your handle is your public identifier on the platform (e.g. `@yourname.celedon.io`). It appears in your profile URL and anywhere your username is shown.

**Changing your handle:**
1. Click the pencil icon next to your current handle.
2. In the dialog, edit the username portion. Your handle will be `{username}.celedon.io`.
3. Click `Save`.

Handles must be unique across the platform.

**Custom Domain Handle (paid plans only):**

Paid users can verify a domain they own and use it as their handle instead of the default `.celedon.io` format. For example, you could use `@alice.example.com` instead of `@alice.celedon.io`.

<Steps>
    <Step title="Start verification">
        Click `Use Custom Domain` in the Handle section. A verification dialog will open.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter your domain">
        Type the domain you want to use (e.g. `example.com`) and click `Continue`.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Prove ownership">
        Davinci will provide verification instructions. You can choose between two methods:

        **DNS Record** — add a TXT record to your domain's DNS with the provided Name/Host and Value. Set the TTL to 3600.

        **HTTPS File** — create a file at a specific URL on your domain with the exact content provided.

        Copy the values using the copy buttons and add them through your domain registrar or hosting provider.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Verify">
        Click `Verify`. Davinci will check for your DNS record or HTTPS file. Once found, your domain is verified and your handle is updated immediately.
    </Step>
</Steps>

If you want to revert to the default `.celedon.io` handle after verifying a custom domain, click `Revert to Default` in the Handle section.

### Notifications

Control which emails you receive from Davinci:
- **Email notifications** — toggle on or off to receive emails for invitations, alerts, and activity updates.
- **Marketing emails** — toggle on or off to receive product updates and news from Celedon Solutions.

### Security

If your deployment has two-factor authentication (2FA) enabled, this section appears with options to:
- **Enable 2FA** — choose between an **Authenticator App** (TOTP) or **Email Code** method. For authenticator apps, scan the QR code and enter the verification code to activate.
- **Disable 2FA** — enter a verification code to turn off two-factor authentication.

### Danger Zone

- **Delete Account** — permanently delete your account and all associated data. You must type `DELETE` to confirm. This action cannot be undone.

<Warning>Deleting your account removes all projects you own and revokes access to shared projects. Export any important data before proceeding.</Warning>

## Billing

The Billing tab shows your subscription and credit information. For a detailed description, see [Accounts, Credits, and Billing](/pages/overview/get-started/account).

Key items on this tab:
- **Subscription Tier** — your current plan
- **Renewal Date** — when your subscription renews (or cancellation date)
- **Upgrade / Change Plan** — link to the [Upgrade page](https://davinci-app.com/~/upgrade)
- **Stripe Account** — click `Manage` to open the Stripe Customer Portal for invoices and payment methods
- **Included Usage** — a gauge showing your remaining subscription credits for the current period
- **On-Demand** — enable or disable on-demand credit usage and set a spending limit
- **Trial Credits** — your remaining trial credit balance
- **Team** — if you are on a team plan, links to the Team hub; otherwise, a link to upgrade to a Team plan

## Connections

The Connections tab is where you manage **Service Accounts** for programmatic access to Davinci.

Service accounts allow automation scripts, AI agents, and CI/CD pipelines to interact with Davinci through the API. Each service account can have multiple API keys with different permission scopes.

- Click `New Service Account` to create one.
- Existing service accounts are listed with their keys and scopes.
- Keys can be created, revoked, or deleted from this tab.

## Storage

The Storage tab gives you an overview of how much storage you are using.

- **Summary** — a line showing your total usage relative to your plan's storage limit.
- **Project breakdown** — a sortable table listing each project's uploaded file size, project name, last accessed date, and project ID. Sort by any column to find your largest files.

Storage limits depend on your plan. See [Individual Plans](/pages/overview/get-started/plans) for the storage included with each tier.

## About

The About tab provides quick links and a way to send feedback.

- **Send Feedback** — type a message and click `Send` to share feedback with the Davinci team.
- **Links** — direct links to the Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Celedon Solutions website, and Davinci documentation.
