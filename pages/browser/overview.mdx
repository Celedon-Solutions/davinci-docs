## Overview

The Davinci Browser is the part of the Davinci app outside the project Editor. Use it to find projects, manage ownership and access, configure your account, respond to notifications, and administer organizations or teams.

## What You Do In The Browser

- Create, import, open, and browse projects.
- View project pages, history, branches, collaborators, and settings.
- Manage your user account, profile, security, connections, and storage.
- Respond to notifications and invitations.
- Manage organizations and teams when you have the required permissions.
- Access billing, usage, storage, admin, and audit surfaces where they apply to your deployment.
