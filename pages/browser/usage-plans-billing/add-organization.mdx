## Overview

Organizations let a group own projects instead of tying important work to a single user account. Organizations can have members, roles, storage controls, and organization-owned projects.

Paid individual tiers can unlock organization creation in self-serve deployments. Team-managed and enterprise deployments may create or assign organizations through team or administrator workflows instead.

## How To Get There

Organization creation and management are available from the Davinci Browser when your account and deployment allow it.

Typical flow:

1. Open the Davinci Browser.
2. Open the organization area or upgrade flow.
3. Create an organization with a name and slug.
4. Configure members, roles, storage, and project ownership.

If you do not see organization creation controls, your plan, team, or deployment may not allow self-service organization creation.

## What Organizations Unlock

Organizations can help you:

- Own projects as a group.
- Keep ownership stable when people change roles.
- Manage organization members and roles.
- Centralize storage and project administration.
- Prepare for organization-scoped automation and API governance as those features are released.

## Billing And Storage

Organization billing depends on how the organization is created and whether it belongs to a team. Some organizations are paid directly; others are managed by team billing or enterprise controls.

See [Organizations](/pages/browser/organizations) for the management model and [Usage, Plans, And Billing](/pages/browser/usage-plans-billing/overview) for usage concepts.
