## Overview

Davinci deployments can be self-serve, enterprise-managed, or privately administered. Because of that, billing is best understood as one part of account, organization, and team management rather than the center of every workflow.

For current public pricing and enterprise options, see [Celedon pricing](https://celedon.solutions/pricing).

## Individual Plans

Individual plans apply to user accounts. Paid individual tiers can unlock higher limits, more included usage, on-demand usage options, more storage, and organization creation.

See [Individual Plans](/pages/getting-started/plans) for the public self-serve plan comparison.

To change an individual plan, see [Change An Individual Plan](/pages/browser/usage-plans-billing/change-individual-plan).

## Included Usage And On-Demand Usage

Included usage is the usage provided with a subscription or deployment allocation. On-demand usage, when available, lets eligible users or teams continue using paid AI features after included usage is exhausted.

Not every deployment exposes the same billing controls. Enterprise deployments may manage usage and access centrally.

## Storage

Storage limits apply to uploaded files and project data. Storage can be associated with a user account, organization, or team depending on who owns the project and how the deployment is configured.

## Organizations

Organizations can own projects, manage members, and carry their own storage and administrative controls. Paid user tiers can unlock organization creation in self-serve deployments.

See [Organizations](/pages/browser/organizations) and [Add An Organization](/pages/browser/usage-plans-billing/add-organization).

## Teams

Teams provide centralized management for users, organizations, projects, usage, billing, and governance. A team can include an organization and can manage usage for multiple members.

See [Teams](/pages/browser/teams) and [Upgrade To A Team](/pages/browser/usage-plans-billing/upgrade-to-team).

## Where To Manage Billing

Depending on your permissions and deployment:

- Individual users manage personal plan and usage from account settings.
- Organization admins manage organization storage and project ownership settings.
- Team admins manage team-level billing, member usage, organization access, and administrative controls.
- Enterprise users may need to contact their system administrator.

## Canceling Or Unsubscribing

Cancellation behavior depends on whether you are managing an individual plan, team, organization, or enterprise deployment. See [Cancel Or Unsubscribe](/pages/browser/usage-plans-billing/cancel-or-unsubscribe).
