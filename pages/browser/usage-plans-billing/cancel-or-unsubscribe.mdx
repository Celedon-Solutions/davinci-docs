## Overview

Cancellation behavior depends on whether you are managing an individual plan, an organization, a team, or an enterprise-managed deployment.

Before canceling, export important data, review project ownership, and make sure you understand what happens to usage, storage, organizations, and team membership.

## Individual Plans

To cancel an individual plan when self-service billing is available:

1. Open the Davinci Browser.
2. Click your avatar.
3. Open **Settings**.
4. Go to **Billing**.
5. Under Subscription click **Manage**
6. Use the Stripe customer portal or cancellation controls available for your plan.

If your account is managed by an enterprise deployment, contact your administrator instead.

## Leaving A Team

If you are a team member but not the team owner, you may be able to leave the team from account or team settings. Leaving a team can change where usage and billing are managed for your account.

## Disbanding A Team

Team owners may be able to disband a team from team settings. Disbanding a team removes team membership and can affect access, billing, included usage, and team-managed organizations.

Disbanding is a high-impact action. Confirm project ownership and exports before proceeding.

## Organizations

Organization billing and storage may be direct, team-managed, or enterprise-managed. Review organization ownership and project ownership before canceling a related plan or storage tier.

## Data Warning

<Warning>
Deleting an account, disbanding a team, deleting an organization, or canceling a plan can affect access to projects and stored data. Export important work before making irreversible changes.
</Warning>

## Need Help?

If you are unsure which cancellation path applies, contact your administrator or Celedon support before making changes.
