## Overview

Teams provide centralized management for users, organizations, usage, billing, and administrative controls. A team can include an organization and can manage usage across multiple members.

Use a team when work should be managed at the group level rather than through individual accounts.

## How To Get There

When self-service team upgrades are available:

1. Open the Davinci Browser.
2. Open account settings or the upgrade flow.
3. Choose the Team upgrade option.
4. Enter a team name and billing frequency.
5. Complete checkout.
6. Open the Team hub after checkout.

Enterprise deployments may provision teams through an administrator instead of the public checkout flow.

## What Teams Manage

Teams can manage:

- Members and invitations.
- Roles and permissions.
- Team usage and spending controls.
- Team billing and subscriptions.
- Organizations associated with the team.
- Projects owned by users or organizations within the team.
- Audit and administrative visibility.

## Team Members

Team members may see different tabs based on permissions, such as Overview, Members, Billing, and Settings.

See [Teams](/pages/browser/teams) for the broader team management model.

## Canceling Or Leaving

If you need to leave or disband a team, review [Cancel Or Unsubscribe](/pages/browser/usage-plans-billing/cancel-or-unsubscribe) and your team settings before making changes.
