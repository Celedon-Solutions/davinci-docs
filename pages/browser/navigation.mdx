## Overview

The Davinci Browser is the part of the Davinci app used to find and manage work around projects. It includes the Home page, project pages, account settings, organizations, teams, notifications, and admin surfaces.

Use the Davinci Browser when you need to manage where work lives, who can access it, or how your account, organization, or team is configured.

## Getting Around

The Browser includes a top bar and navigation menu. Depending on your screen size and deployment, some links appear in the top bar and others appear in the burger menu.

Common navigation destinations include:

- **Home / Projects** — view projects you own or can access.
- **Organizations** — manage organization-owned projects, members, storage, and settings.
- **Teams** — manage team members, usage, organizations, and administrative settings.
- **Notifications** — review invites, announcements, account events, and system messages.
- **Account Settings** — manage your profile, security, connections, storage, and personal settings.
- **Docs** — open the Davinci documentation.

## How To Reach Common Pages

- To find a project, go to **Home** and select it from the project list.
- To open the Editor, open a project page and click **Open Project**.
- To manage your account, click your avatar and choose **Settings**.
- To view notifications, click the notification bell or open Notifications from the menu.
- To manage an organization, open the organization page from the Browser or from a project owned by that organization.
- To manage a team, open the Team hub from the Browser navigation.
