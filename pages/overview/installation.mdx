## Minimum System Requirements
- **Operating System:** Windows 10/11 with Administrator privileges
- **Processor:** Intel Pentium 4 or AMD Athlon 64 Processor
- **Memory:** 8 GB RAM
- **Hard Drive Space:** 500 MB of free disk space
- **Graphics:** Integrated graphics
- **Display:** 1280 x 720 screen resolution or greater
- **Network:** Internet connection for installation, updates, and usage

## Step-by-Step Installation Instructions

1. **Download the Installer**
   - Click on the download link in your confirmation email.
   - Click on the download button on the opened webpage.

2. **Run the Installer**
   - Locate the downloaded installer file and double-click to run it.
   - Follow the on-screen prompts to proceed with the installation.
   - Wait for the installation process to complete.

3. **Launch Davinci Beta**
   - Once the installation is complete, launch Davinci Beta from the Start menu or desktop shortcut.

4. **Activate the License**
   - Copy your license key provided in your confirmation email.
   - On the Davinci home screen, locate the license key input field.
   - Paste your license key into the field.
   - Click the "Validate" button to activate your license.

## Uninstalling Davinci
- Go to 'Add or remove programs' in your system settings
- Locate and select the Davinci Beta application
- Click uninstall
- Follow the on-screen uninstaller prompts
