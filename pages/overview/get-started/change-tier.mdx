---
title: Change Tier or Payment Info
---

If you already have a Davinci Account, you can update your current subscription tier or payment info any time by following these steps:

## Managing your Subscription from Davinci
<Steps>
    <Step title="Log in to Davinci">
        Visit [https://davinci-app.com/account](https://davinci-app.com/account) and log in. This should open your User Account.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Access your Stripe Account">
        Click on `Manage Subscription` in the Account Screen. You will be redirected to a Stripe Subscription Management page.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Update Your Subscription">
        From within Stripe, click the `Update Subscription` button in the top right of the screen. Continue following prompts from Stripe until you have successfully changed your subscription to the desired tier.
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing your Subscription from Stripe
<Steps>
    <Step title="Log in to Davinci">
        Visit [The Celedon Solutions Stripe Customer Portal](https://pay.celedon.solutions/p/login/eVacOJ0ZDfWvdR65kk) and log in with the same email you used to purchase your Davinci subscription.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Check your email">
        Stripe will email you a link that you can use to access your account details. Click on this link to access your account.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Update Your Subscription">
        From within Stripe, click the `Update Subscription` button in the top right of the screen. Continue following prompts from Stripe until you have successfully changed your subscription to the desired tier.
    </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens When You Upgrade Tiers
- Subscription Upgrades will be charged immediately for the new tier, prorated based on the previous tier value. 
- Your new Subscription Credits will be added to your account immediately.

## What Happens When You Downgrade Tiers
- Subscription Downgrades will also occur immediately. 
- You will receive a pro-rated credit to your account based on previous usage of the higher tier plan.


## Enterprise Users
For Enterprise Users, please contact support at [support@celedon.solutions](mailto:support@celedon.solutions) to change your contract or subscription.



<Warning>If your account expires or is deleted, all project data will be removed from our servers. This is an unrecoverable operation. Be sure to export any important data before Unsubscribing from Davinci.</Warning>
