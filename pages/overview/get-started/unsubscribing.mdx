---
title: Unsubscribing
---

We hate to see you go! But if you need to unsubscribe, follow these steps below:

## Unsubscribing from Davinci using Davinci

<Steps>
    <Step title="Open Billing Settings">
        Go to [https://davinci-app.com/~/settings/billing](https://davinci-app.com/~/settings/billing).
    </Step>
    <Step title="Access your Stripe Account">
        Click the `Manage` button next to Stripe Account. You will be redirected to the Stripe Customer Portal.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Cancel Your Subscription">
        From within Stripe, click the Cancel Subscription button. Continue following prompts from Stripe until you have successfully cancelled your subscription.
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Unsubscribing from Davinci using Stripe
<Steps>
    <Step title="Log in to Stripe">
        Visit [The Celedon Solutions Stripe Customer Portal](https://pay.celedon.solutions/p/login/eVacOJ0ZDfWvdR65kk) and log in with the same email you used to purchase your Davinci subscription.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Check your email">
        Stripe will email you a link that you can use to access your account details. Click on this link to access your account.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Cancel Your Subscription">
        From within Stripe, click the Cancel Subscription button. Continue following prompts from Stripe until you have successfully cancelled your subscription.
    </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>Cancelled subscriptions will expire at the end of the current billing period. You will retain access to your account until the billing date listed in the Billing tab of your Account Settings.</Note>


## Enterprise Users
For Enterprise Users, please contact support at [support@celedon.solutions](mailto:support@celedon.solutions) to unsubscribe.



<Warning>Once your account expires, all project data will be deleted. This is an unrecoverable operation. Be sure to export any important data before Unsubscribing from Davinci.</Warning>
