## Version 4.5.1 - Trial Teams & Team Billing

This release improves organization billing transitions and fixes child-field selection in Matrix filters.

### Organizations & Upgrades

- Added billing upgrade prompts for standalone organizations that require a subscription
- Added grace-period warnings and a direct **Upgrade Billing** action
- Preserved organizations with a billing grace period when a trial team ends or is disbanded
- Fixed links between team, organization, and account billing pages

### Matrix Views

- Fixed Matrix filter **Children** steps so Attribute and Constraint fields are available when building child-based filters

### Reliability

- Improved trial expiration, billing status, and upgrade handling across team and organization workflows
