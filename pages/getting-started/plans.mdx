Davinci offers three individual plans. All plans include access to the full modeling platform — the difference is in credits, storage, project limits, and AI model quality.

To view current pricing or change your plan, visit the Upgrade page at `/~/upgrade`.

## Plan Comparison

| | **Free** | **Core** | **Max** |
|---|----------|----------|---------|
| **Included Credits** | Trial credits on signup | Replenished monthly or annually | Replenished monthly or annually |
| **AI Model Quality** | Standard | Standard | Premium |
| **Owned Projects** | 2 | 5 | Unlimited |
| **Storage** | 100 MB | 1 GB | 10 GB |
| **On-Demand Credits** | Not available | Available | Available |
| **Price** | Free | [See upgrade page](/pages/browser/change-plan) | [See upgrade page](/pages/browser/change-plan) |

## Free

The Free plan is a great way to get started with Davinci at no cost. When you create an account, you receive a set of trial credits to explore the platform.

- **Trial Credits** — a one-time pool of credits provided at signup. Trial credits do not expire and are consumed after any included subscription credits are used.
- **Standard AI Models** — access to capable AI models for design tasks.
- **2 Owned Projects** — create up to 2 projects that you own.
- **100 MB Storage** — for uploaded reference files and project data.

The Free plan does not include monthly credit replenishment or on-demand credit purchasing.

## Core

The Core plan is designed for active individual users who need regular credit replenishment and more project capacity.

- **Monthly Credit Replenishment** — your included credits are refilled on each billing date, up to the total provided by your plan. Credits do not roll over between billing periods.
- **Standard AI Models** — the same AI model tier as the Free plan.
- **5 Owned Projects** — create up to 5 projects that you own.
- **1 GB Storage** — ten times the storage of the Free plan.
- **On-Demand Credits** — optionally enable on-demand spending to continue using credits beyond your included allocation. On-demand usage is billed at the per-credit rate for your plan.

Core is available in monthly and yearly billing frequencies. Yearly plans include more total credits for the year at a lower effective rate.

## Max

The Max plan is for power users who need the best AI models, unlimited projects, and generous storage.

- **Monthly Credit Replenishment** — significantly more included credits than Core, replenished on each billing date.
- **Premium AI Models** — access to the highest-quality AI models available in Davinci, including advanced providers for design tasks, CAD authoring, and complex reasoning.
- **Unlimited Owned Projects** — no cap on the number of projects you can create.
- **10 GB Storage** — ample space for large models with many reference files.
- **On-Demand Credits** — same on-demand option as Core, with the same per-credit rate.

Max is available in monthly and yearly billing frequencies.

## Credits

All plans use credits for AI-powered operations such as generating model objects, chatting with the design agent, and uploading files. For details on how credits work, including the difference between included usage, on-demand credits, and trial credits, see [Accounts, Credits, and Billing](/pages/browser/usage-plans-billing/overview).

## Upgrading and Downgrading

You can change your plan at any time from the Upgrade page at `/~/upgrade` or from your Billing settings at `/~/settings/billing`. For step-by-step instructions, see [Change Tier or Payment Info](/pages/browser/change-plan).

## Teams

If you need shared billing, centralized spending controls, and member management, consider upgrading to a Team plan instead. Team plans replace your individual subscription. See [Teams](/pages/browser/teams) for details.
