## What is Davinci?

Davinci is an automated digital engineering tool that transforms user inputs into complete models through natural language. It is your AI design assistant. 

## How does it work?

Davinci is a software service provided by Celedon Solutions that connects users via a desktop client to our powerful AI service that automates modeling. The client connects to both the AI service and a database that hosts your project data.  

## What can it do?

Checkout the features page to see all of the things Davinci can do. Davinci is currently in beta, so check back regularly for updates and new features.
